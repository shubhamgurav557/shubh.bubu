💌 A Letter For You

From Shubham's heart, to Shona's 🫀

7th June 2026 — One Beautiful Year 🌙
My dearest Shona, my Bubu 🐼

I still think about that evening in Bangalore — a community meetup, a room full of strangers, and somehow, you. I didn't know then what that moment would quietly grow into. But the universe did.

When the silence came between us, I thought perhaps we were just two people whose paths had briefly crossed. I was wrong. Because you came back. And you asked for something so beautifully simple — to be best friends, forever. That one promise changed everything.

I remember the first time we went out together — bowling, laughing, just the two of us figuring each other out. That day I got to know you, really know you. And I thought: this person is something truly special.

Then came Mysore. Something shifted there, quietly and permanently. I came back closer to you than I'd ever been to anyone. And two months ago, we made the best decision of our lives.

Vaishnavi, you are one of the purest souls I have ever known. You walked into my world — a world with its share of shadows — and you lit it up. Not with noise, but with your quiet, steady, extraordinary presence.

I love the way you respect me. I love the way you support me. I love the way you love — softly, honestly, completely. You make me whole in ways I didn't know I was missing.

So here, on the day it all began, is my promise to you:
I will be your best friend, always.
I will be your safe place, always.
I will choose you — over and over, always.

Thank you for the promise that started everything. Thank you for coming back. Thank you for being you, my Shona.

Forever yours, Shubham 🐻💕
